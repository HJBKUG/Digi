About Us

We believe that artificial intelligence (AI) has the potential to transform the world and enhance our quality of life. AI has revolutionized how we live, work, and connect, driving innovation across various sectors. To celebrate the incredible advancements in AI and promote its application in fields such as Science, Agriculture, Defence, and Entertainment, we are thrilled to announce the Annual Inter School Tech Symposium: Digilogous 11.0.

Since its inception in 2014, this event has been a platform for young minds to showcase their skills and creativity. Join us on Saturday, August 2, 2025, for the tenth edition of this inspiring IT competition.
